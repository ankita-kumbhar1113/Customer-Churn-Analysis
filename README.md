# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis | End-to-End Data Analytics Project

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn for a telecom company using **SQL, Python and Power BI**. It identifies key churn drivers, customer risk segments, and revenue impact through interactive dashboards. The analysis concludes with data-driven business recommendations to improve customer retention and support decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
A telecom company wants to understand:

- Why are customers leaving?
- Which customers are at high risk?
- What factors drive churn?
- How can churn be reduced?

# 📊 Key Insights

- Month-to-Month customers have the highest churn.
- Fiber Optic customers are more likely to churn.
- Customers with high monthly charges show higher churn.
- New customers (0–12 months) are at the highest risk.
- Customers without Tech Support and Online Security churn more frequently.
- ₹2.86M revenue is at risk due to customer churn.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Convert Month-to-Month customers to long-term contracts.
- Improve onboarding for new customers.
- Bundle Tech Support and Online Security services.
- Improve Fiber Optic service quality.
- Launch targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
